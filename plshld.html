♦ Concept

Corresponding to that only mouths of the actors will appear in the film. Eyes, “the windows to the soul,” in the icon is also eliminated as a metaphor of the value of the company. Designed for consistent propaganda posters, ID sticker*, countdown timer*, and index cards*.
*not applied in the final output due to the change in shooting location and accessibility of materials.

♦ Set Design

We researched into the Dystopian aesthetic and built the scene with 90s call center cubicle.

The film, orriginally planned to be shooted in a cubic, was interrupted by the COVID19 pandemic, and modified to adapt the actress's home webcam.
