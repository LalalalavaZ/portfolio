CWCS

Conversation Without Common Sense (CWCS)

An experiment on the language, mechanism, and interface design of popular human-mimicking chatbots. This project aims to research and explore what kind of communication model a machine can generate without learning from human “common sense”.
The detailed timeline and project management can be found here on the notion page.
