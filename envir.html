Halloween Party Projection

2019 Fall
Projected film from motion captured data
An animation of skeletons dancing under a green sun, later projected in loop to the ceiling of an apartment during a Halloween Party. There are barely projection art on the ceiling, because human naturally don’t tend to look up. But this work performed wonderfully in building the mood, fixing text-neck, and not taking the too much attention from “making connection” in the party.
